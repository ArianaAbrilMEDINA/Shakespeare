The Characters

Macbeth

He is a strong man, with an imposing bearing and a determined gaze. He acts bravely in battle, but his attitude changes: he becomes ambitious, impulsive, and increasingly paranoid

Lady Macbeth

She has an elegant and firm presence, with an intense gaze. She is domineering, cold, and determined, though she later becomes fragile and consumed by guilt.

Banquo

He is a robust and serious soldier, with a firm expression. He is prudent, loyal, and thoughtful, remaining upright even in the face of ambition.

The witches

They have a strange appearance: pale skin, messy hair, and beards. They are mysterious, unsettling, and manipulative, always speaking in riddles.

The desire for power

When the first part of the prophecy comes true, Macbeth begins to feel an ambition he had never recognized before.
Lady Macbeth wants the prophecy to come true by murdering King Duncan to gain more power.
